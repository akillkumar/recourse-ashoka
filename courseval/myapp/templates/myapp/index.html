How our ratings work



Each course is given three metrics:

  1. Rating: Overall rating of the course (out of 5).
  2. Difficulty: The difficulty of the course in terms of material/exams and scoring (out of 5).
  3. Retake %: The percentage of students who would retake the course.


Professor ratings are averages of all the courses they offer.



Ratings can only be submitted by users with an Ashoka email ID.
